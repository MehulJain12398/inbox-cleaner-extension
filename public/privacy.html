Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

Inbox Cleaner AI is a Chrome Extension that connects with your Gmail account to help you categorize, clean, and summarize your inbox using AI. Your privacy is extremely important to us.

What We Collect

  • Email metadata (subject, sender, date, and labels)
  • Email snippets (only if user grants access)
  • No full email body is stored or shared

How We Use the Data

  • To show you categorized email summaries
  • To enable AI-based suggestions and cleanup actions
  • To improve our features (aggregate analytics only)

Data Security

We use Google's OAuth 2.0 for authentication. Your access tokens are stored securely in your browser and are never shared with third parties.

Third-Party APIs

This extension uses the Gmail API and OpenAI API to provide services. We do not store or misuse your email data.

Your Consent

By using this extension, you consent to our privacy policy.
